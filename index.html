I will help you if you're not happy with the way you look.

Free Consultation

The first step is for us to talk about what you're looking for and how I can help you. We can meet in person or catch up over the phone, at no cost to you. If you decide to go ahead, we'll agree which of the one to one sessions will be best for you and what your budget is.

One to One Sessions

Wardrobe wonders

After this session, you'll know what suits your body shape and colouring and how to coordinate and accessorise your existing wardrobe. We'll rediscover any forgotten gems and decide what could be altered to give it new life. I'll take everything else away and donate it to charity (so you don't have to). By the end, you'll have a beautifully organised wardrobe that you know how to wear - perfect! This takes between 3-4 hours and costs £150.

Shopping together

In advance of our day together I'll source wonderful clothes and accessories that will suit you, meet your brief and keep you within budget. On the day there's no hassle, no wasted time and always a good place to refuel with a bite to eat and a coffee. All you have to do is relax and enjoy your transformation. This takes about six hours, including a break for lunch and costs £275. If you have limited time or a small shopping list, we can have a half-day session for £150.

Shopping for you

If you don't have time or the inclination for a day's shopping, I can source everything based on your budget, what you need and when you need it by, and bring it to your home or office. The pricing for this service depends on your brief and timeline.

Styling your wedding

Weddings can be stressful and mothers of the bride and groom in particular can really worry about looking their best on the day. I'll work with your wedding party to make sure they all look and feel good on the day and make sure everyone's choices complement each other. The cost of this session depends on the number of people involved but the costs are the same rate as other sessions. There's no stealth wedding tax!

About

Who is Miss Harding?

I love personal styling because whether I've spent an afternoon with a client sorting through their wardrobe or we've enjoyed a day together buying beautiful clothes, they're always so amazed and excited at the change in how they look. In some ways nothing's really changed, it's still them, with the same body and the same doubts but somehow looking good has the power to make people feel better. People have different abilities and mine is that I have a natural eye for style - I can look at something and just know if the pattern, cut or combination is right. And after many years of looking for the best and most interesting shops, I know where to find things too! From a personal perspective, after an illness and surgery some years ago, I was grateful for this skill and came to a deeper appreciation of the power of looking good when you're not necessarily feeling it. So I don't focus on the latest fashions in the sense of what we 'should' be wearing. I'm more interested in finding my clients the best, most beautiful and most flattering clothes and accessories for them. And making sure the process is fun and transformative. If you decide to work with me, I'll do this for you too. Please do get in touch and we can talk about what you want and take it from there.
